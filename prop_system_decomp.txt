Power Supply Subsystem

Power Supply Failure
- Battery Failure
  - Cell capacity degradation (OR) [2]
  - Decrease in Li-Ion concentration (OR)
  - Short circuit (OR)
    - Material flaw
    - Aging
    - Vibration
    - Dendrites
  - BMS comms fault (OR)
  - Connector corrosion
  - Thermal runaway (OR)
    - Overcharging
    - Aging

- Power Supply Module Failure (OR)
  - Voltage regulation failure (OR)
    - Component wear
    - Thermal stress
  - Overvoltage/undervoltage output (OR)
    - Regulator failure
    - Load transient

- Power Distribution/Conversion Failure
  - DC-DC converter fault (OR)
    - Transformer saturation
    - Switching FET
  - Load rejection device (OR) [3]
  - Overload/overcurrent condition (AND)
    - Short circuit
    - Protection device fails

- Cable/Connector Failure
  - Cable insulation corrosion (OR)
    - Seawater corrosion
    - Abrasion
  - Connector corrosion (OR)
    - Mate/demate
    - Inadequate sealing
  - Mechanical cable break (OR)
    - Vibration fatigue
    - Impact


Thruster Subsystem Failure
- Motor Failure
  - Winding short circuit failure (OR)
    - Insulation breakdown
    - Overload
    - Water ingress
  - Bearing seizure/mechanical wear (OR)
    - Lubrication loss
    - Overload
    - Sand/silt ingress
  - Overheating (OR)
    - Prolonged high-current demand
    - Poor thermal path
  - Water ingress (OR) [2]
    - Seal failure
    - Housing breach
    - Pressure exceedance

- Prop Failure
  - Blade deformation/fracture (OR) [2]
    - Obstacle impact
    - Cavitation
    - Fatigue
  - Foreign object entanglement (OR) [1]
    - Marine debris
    - Kelp
    - Fishing gear
    - Monofilament line
  - Loosening/detachment from shaft (OR)
    - Fastener fatigue
    - Vibration
    - Inadequate torquing

- Shaft/Seal Failure
  - Shaft misalignment (OR)
    - Impact
    - Assembly error
  - Shaft bearing failure (OR)
    - Lubrication loss
    - Contamination
    - Overload
  - Seal Failure (AND)
    - Pressure differential exceedance
    - Seal degradation
    - Mechanical failure from impact

- Housing/Interface Failure
  - Housing breach
    - Obstacle impact
    - Manufacturing defect
    - Fatigue crack
    - Pressure cycling
  - O-ring/gasket issues
    - Improper assembly
    - O-ring aging
    - Surface defect
  - Thruster Interface Connector
    - Connector pin failure
    - Mechanical damage
    - Excessive mating cycles
    - Corrosion
